About KreativTools

A practical briefing desk for business readers.

KreativTools helps readers understand the business, market, technology, and regional signals that matter without opening ten tabs first.

Source layer

Public sources, clearly attributed

KreativTools uses public feeds as discovery signals, keeps source attribution visible, and sends readers to the original publisher for full reporting.

Reader flow

Start with a short editorial brief

The homepage starts with an original editorial note, then a small set of curated cards and fixed topic pages for AI, markets, Europe, and Romania.

Product direction

Signal over feed volume

The goal is not to copy publisher sites or become an infinite feed. It is to add a useful scan layer that helps readers decide what deserves more time.

Editorial standard

Original context comes first.

KreativTools summarizes signals in its own words, avoids republishing full articles, and links readers to the original publisher whenever they want the full story.
