    About SignalLedger

    A fast news desk for business readers.

    SignalLedger is a prototype briefing product for people who want market, technology, business, and geopolitical context without opening ten tabs first.

    Source layer

    Google News RSS

    The prototype collects current headlines from Google News RSS, keeps source attribution visible, and links every card back to the original publisher.

    Reader control

    Prefer or hide sources

    Local source preferences let readers shape the feed without needing an account during the prototype phase.

    Product direction

    Signal over volume

    The goal is not to be another infinite feed. It is to package the few stories that deserve a decision maker's attention.

    What comes next

    Editorial differentiation matters.

    The durable version of this product needs stronger curation, proprietary summaries, better market data, and an email habit. The current site is the local prototype for proving that direction.
