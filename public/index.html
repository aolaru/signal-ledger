About SignalLedger

A fast news desk for business readers.

SignalLedger is a briefing product for people who want market, technology, business, and geopolitical context without opening ten tabs first.

Source layer

Direct publisher feeds first

SignalLedger uses public RSS feeds as discovery signals, keeps source attribution visible, and sends readers to the original publisher for the full reporting.

Reader flow

Start with the strongest brief

The product now favors a short daily briefing first, then topic pages for readers who want to go deeper on AI, markets, Europe, Romania, or another search.

Product direction

Signal over feed volume

The goal is not to be another infinite feed. It is to package the few stories that deserve a business reader's attention, with original brief pages and clearer source context.

What comes next

Editorial differentiation matters.

The durable version of this product needs stronger curation, cleaner source quality, better market data, and a repeatable email habit. The current site is the working product foundation for that direction.
